Pizza Recipe

Home

A delicious cheesy pizza that is fit for an ocassion

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Water
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chicken

    • Steps

    1. Get water and flour and mix them together into a ball
    2. Once in a ball, put it in the fridge in a bowl with plastic wrap over it
    3. Take it out of the fridge and roll it out into a circle
    4. Put tomato sauce on the pizza and spread it evenly
    5. Put cheese on the pizza and spread it out evenly
    6. Put the chicken on the pizza evenly
    7. Once ready, put it in the oven and wait
    8. Take it out and enjoy
